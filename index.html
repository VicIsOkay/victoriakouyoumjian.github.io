About

I'm a product marketing executive with extensive experience translating technically complex products into differentiated positioning, compelling market narratives, and revenue-driving go-to-market programs.

My career spans some of the most consequential product launches in enterprise technology — including the GA launch of Amazon SageMaker and Amazon Lex at AWS, Intel's Enterprise AI go-to-market, and building Sprinklr's Platform PMM function from zero. I'm a rapid domain learner with a track record of entering unfamiliar verticals and quickly developing authoritative points of view.

I hold an MBA from the University of Redlands, a B.S. in Geography (GIS) from the University of Wyoming, and a B.A. in English Literature from Mount Holyoke College.
